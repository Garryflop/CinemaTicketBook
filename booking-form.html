Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book tickets online?

+

Select your movie, choose your preferred showtime, pick your seats, and complete the payment. You'll receive a confirmation email with your e-tickets immediately after booking.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

+

Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking up to 2 hours before the showtime. A cancellation fee may apply. Contact our customer service for assistance at +7 (747) 174-25-08.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Kaspi, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are secure and encrypted with bank-level SSL security.

Do you offer group discounts?

+

Yes! Groups of 10 or more people can enjoy special discounted rates. Please contact us at least 24 hours in advance to arrange group bookings and receive a custom quote.

What should I bring to the cinema?

+

Bring your booking confirmation (digital or printed), a valid ID, and arrive 15 minutes before showtime. Outside food and beverages are not permitted in our cinemas.

Are there age restrictions for movies?

+

Yes, we follow standard movie ratings (G, PG, PG-13, R). Children under 13 must be accompanied by an adult for PG-13 movies. R-rated movies require valid ID for entry.
